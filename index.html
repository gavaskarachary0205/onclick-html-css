Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is considered to be the greatest architectural achievement in the whole range of Indo-Islamic architecture. Not a piece of architecture, as other buildings are, but the proud passions of an emperors love wrought in living stones.

Golden Temple

The Golden Temple, also known as Harmandir Sahib, meaning "abode of God", is a gurdwara located in the city of Amritsar, Punjab, India.The gurdwara is built around a man-made pool (sarovar) that was completed by the fourth Sikh Guru, Guru Ram Das in 1577.
